Test Prints

This is the DC motor that I'll be using to drive my turbine. I did some measurements with a vernier but I'll also have to do some test prints to ensure that my measurements are correct as well as the printer's tolerances/dimensions.

I created a couple of test prints for the external housing for the DC motor for it to fit in as well as the shaft for the blades to fit on.

Even though the measured diameter of the DC shaft was 2mm, none of the test prints were able to fit, I created another batch of test prints with slightly larger diameters. This time when printing, I opted to print them on a raft such that they would stick together as a batch, making it easier to organise/keep track off compared to the loose pieces.
